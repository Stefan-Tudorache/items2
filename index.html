Boots Of Speed

As you defeat the fire lion and the flames grow cold, a pair of boots spring from the ground like magic. You immediately recognize them from an old legend, they are the boots of a druid that could took the shape of any beast and once guarded a vast and beautiful forest at the edge of this plains.

+2 movement speed for every roll in Plains of Ithilien and Dark Woods.

Book of Knowledge

Stories, past experiences or simple information about every place or monster found in the world, makes this a very valuable book, since it can tell you how to easily defeat even the strongest foes. It not for nothing they say that "Knowledge is Power". Single Use:

Defeat the Hydra with no effort. +5 Power against any dragon. +4 Power against Lilith, the spider queen.

Oathkeeper

The order of the Silver Hand was a group of knights who trained all their lives to hunt and defeat the dragons. This days, only stories remain and the sword of a captain that swore to keep his oath even in death. The sword is glowing and seems to be charged with magic, maybe the captain is still keeping his promise.

+2 Power against monsters and PvP. +4 Power against any dragon.

Renfri

A bad decision after another has put Renfri on the path of thieving and evil deeds. However after learning about your quest, she offers you her help, in the search for forgiveness. A skilled warrior and deadly with a bow, she will now fight by your side in time of need.

+2 Power in PvP. +3 Power against any monster.

Elven Dagger

Soon as you see it, you know that this is a dagger from the long lost elves. No one really knows why they are gone or where, but tales of their beauty, remnants of their city and statues can be found all over Ithilien. Some say that they possessed the power over nature and they are still with us in every tree and flower,but nobody knows for sure.

+3 power in PvP. +3 power against any monster.

Polaris Amulet

Born from the light of the north star, many think is a cold and distant light, but those who felt it know it is memory, precious and pure of a promise to return home even from the darkest night, and so the light of Polaris will be your best friend in to the Dark Woods.

With Polaris Amulet you will never get lost after every 3 turns in the Dark Woods.

Lake of Atlantis

Legend says that long long time ago there was no lake, but a beautiful and prosperous city. The dragons became envious and so with their power and magic submerge the city to be lost forever.Now only the ruins can be found and if you don;t know the way, you can sink your boat before you know it.

The map shows you a safe path, thus the ruins can't harm your boat anymore, except the monster fights.

Spider Venom

Spiders like to "play" with their victims, but to do that, they first need to weaken the prey by poisoning it. After defeating the queen, you decide to gather her venom in the hopes that it will be of help later on.

Poison a monster by 2 ( +2 power for you in a fight ).Cannot be used in fire land. PvP : after wining a fight, poison the oponent (-4 movement speed for next 3 rolls)

Balrog Scale

Legend says that a group of brave heroes fought the fire dragon. Although defeated, they managed to harm the Ashbringer, making him to lose one scale. A skilled blacksmith then turned it into an amulet. Warm to the touch it seems you can never get cold while you wear it.

The amulet protects you from the cold effects of the ice region (Cancel the speed penalty).

Having heard the stories of what beautiful and good place the forest once was, you find it hard to believe that now everything is tainted with darkness and is hard to know anymore what is good or bad in this place.
